Hangout Finder App! This is made to help friends / traveling groups / etc to find things to do around 6 major cities. 
Pick a city, and choose a catergoy. Cafe, chill, arcade, or outdoor activies! This will bring down a list of a bunch of things to do. 
If you dont want to pick, just click the Choose Random Hangout of the Day! Let it plan your next trip!
